Data Resampling and Alignment

The Solar Forecast Arbiter's preprocessing applies the above validation and excludes (other options to be added in the future) the data. The preprocessing then resamples the observations to the forecast using the mean. Then it checks for undefined values, such as not-a-number (nan) values and removes them.
